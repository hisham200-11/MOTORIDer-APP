MotoRide Help Center

Everything you need to get started and ride safely.

MotoRide is a motorcycle ride-hailing platform. Choose your role below to see how to get started.
Passenger
  1. 1
    Click "Rider Mode"

    Takes you to the Passenger login page.

  2. 2
    Click "Sign up" if new

    Fill in name, phone, GCash number, username, and password.

  3. 3
    Log in and book a ride

    Enter credentials → Sign In → you're on your dashboard.

Driver
  1. 1
    Click "Driver Mode"

    Takes you to the Driver login page.

  2. 2
    Click "Sign up" if new

    Fill in personal info, vehicle details, driver license, and GCash number.

  3. 3
    Log in and go online

    Toggle status to Online to start accepting rides.

Phone numbers must be numbers only — no dashes or spaces. Duplicate usernames or phone numbers are not allowed.
Booking a Ride — Step by Step
  1. 1
    Set Pickup Location

    The map auto-detects your GPS. Click the map (first click = green pin) or type in the Pickup box.

  2. 2
    Set Dropoff Location

    Second map click = red dropoff pin. Route and estimated fare appear automatically.

  3. 3
    Choose Payment Method

    Select Cash or GCash. With GCash, your wallet balance is shown. If balance < fare, top up first or switch to Cash.

  4. 4
    Click "Find Available Drivers"

    A list of online drivers appears with name, vehicle brand, model, and color.

  5. 5
    Click "Accept" on a Driver

    Ride is submitted as Pending. Once the driver confirms, status changes to "Driver is on the way!"

  6. 6
    Ride In Progress → Completed

    When the driver starts the trip, status shows "Ride in Progress 🚀". When done, a full receipt appears — click Done to close.

Use Clear & Reset to start over, or Find My Location to re-center the map on your GPS position.
Going Online & Accepting Rides
  1. 1
    Toggle Your Status to Online

    On your dashboard, flip the toggle switch. You'll appear in the available drivers list for passengers.

  2. 2
    Accept an Incoming Request

    Requests appear in "Incoming Requests" with passenger name, pickup/dropoff, distance, fare, and payment method.

  3. 3
    Start the Ride

    After picking up the passenger, click 🚀 Start Ride.

  4. 4
    Complete the Ride

    At the destination, click ✅ Complete Ride → confirm. A receipt shows gross fare, 12% platform fee, and your net earnings.

Your Total Rides and Total Earnings update in real time. Full breakdowns are in the History tab.
